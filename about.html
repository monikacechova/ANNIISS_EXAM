ANNIISS is a symbolic character of the wrongly persecuted, based on the folklore and real-life story of the last woman to be convicted of witchcraft and consequently killed, during the Danish witch-hunt. Anniiss was a woman of nature; a wise-woman who understood the power of Mother Earth at the time of the arrival of Christianity in Scandinavia. Pagan wise-women were captured, tortured, and forced to admit to being in touch with the devil. Anniiss was one of these women. Publicly defiant against her accusers until the end, she was beheaded and burned. Out of the ashes arose the legend of Anniiss - a name she allegedly got from the devil who showed himself to her as a black cat. Today, hearing the call of the times, ANNIISS has risen from the flames to give a voice to the unheard and challenge the norms of society.
